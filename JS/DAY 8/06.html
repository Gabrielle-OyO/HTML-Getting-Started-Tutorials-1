limitTo过滤器

原字符串：{{“Hello world"}}

{{“Hello world" | limitTo:2}}

{{“Hello world" | limitTo:-2}}

{{“Hello world" | limitTo66}}

{{“Hello world" | limitTo:2:6}}


截取前2个字符，然后变为大写：{{"Hello World"|limitTo:2|uppercase}}


{{[3,7,12,4] | limitTo : 2}}

{{[3,7,12,4] | limitTo : -2}}

{{[3,7,12,4] | limitTo : 2:3}}
